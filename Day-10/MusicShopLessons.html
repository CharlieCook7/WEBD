Lessons

Cook music offers lessons for guitar, violin, trumpet. trombone, flute, and clarinet, regardless of your skill level. Our instructors are ready to work whatever date and time works best for you. Whether you come to us for your lessons, or we come to you, we are dedicated to make lessons as convenient for you as possible.

Disclaimer: Our instructors have busy lives, and something may come that will force them to cancel a lesson. If this happens, we will try our best to send in a substitute instructor, but cannot always guarantee somebody will be able to fill in.
