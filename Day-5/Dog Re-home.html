Re-Home a Dog

ARS Shelters have plenty of canine companions just waiting for a new home.
Here are some that you can take with you to give a forever home:


Marley is a 4-year-old shih tzu. She's often afraid of new people,
but once she warms up to you, she loves to hang around with
you and follow you around all day


Spike is a 7-year-old pitbull. He likes his space before he warms
up to you, and might act a little aggressive if you don't give it to him,
but once he's used to you, he's one of the most gentle and
affectionate dogs you've ever met.


Max is a 6-year-old mixed breed dog. Though sometimes he'll
cuddle right up to you, he often enjoys to be left to his own
devices. Just watch he doesn't get into any mischief!
